Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: 1:1 Japanese job recruitment banner for Jimoty, PNG raster image

Reusable prompt:
Create a high-impact Japanese job recruitment banner with a bright pachislot / jackpot advertising mood, adapted for a trustworthy Jimoty job post. The image should feel energetic, shiny, and high-income oriented, but not like a button ad or scammy CTA.

Main text hierarchy:
- Largest and most important text: 「{{salary_text}}」
- Supporting headline: 「{{role_phrase}}」
- Small trust/entry label: 「未経験OK」
- Optional location/support text if needed: 「{{region}}周辺」 or a short neutral area label using 「{{region}}」

Layout:
- Square 1:1 composition with dense, celebratory visual energy.
- Put 「{{salary_text}}」 in the center as the dominant focal point, using very large, thick, readable Japanese typography.
- Place 「{{role_phrase}}」 above or near the salary as a clear job-category headline.
- Add 1-2 small benefit badges only, such as 「未経験OK」 and 「安定」. Keep badges secondary to the salary.
- Use a layered foreground/midground/background structure: workers or job-related scene at the sides or upper corners, bold salary text in front, sparkles and confetti behind.
- Avoid cluttering the bottom with CTA-style copy. The bottom may contain decorative coins, light streaks, or a short neutral benefit label, but no call-to-action wording.

Visual style:
- Photo-real or semi-real raster background with strong depth, lighting, texture, and atmosphere.
- Use a glossy entertainment-ad style: gold bevels, red outlines, black drop shadows, orange rim lights, starburst highlights, confetti, lens flares, and sparkling reflections.
- Typography should be bold, 3D, high-contrast, and readable on a smartphone. Use gold/yellow fills, red or black outline layers, and bright highlights.
- Overall palette: gold, red, orange, black, deep blue, and small rainbow confetti accents. Keep the salary text bright and highly legible.
- If people are shown, make them approachable Japanese job workers in workwear or office/work-support attire that matches 「{{role_phrase}}」. Expressions can be positive and energetic, but not exaggerated into an unsafe or misleading tone.
- The scene should suggest a clean Japanese recruitment banner mood: approachable, active, trustworthy, and income-focused.

Text rules:
- Keep all visible Japanese text short and hierarchy-driven.
- Do not include LINE URLs, QR codes, company names, real brand logos, watermarks, or tiny unreadable text.
- Do not make 「クリックして」「ボタンで」「LINEで」「今すぐ応募」「GETだぜ」 or similar action-button CTA wording the main message.
- Do not contradict the job post. Use the placeholders exactly where dynamic text is needed: 「{{role_phrase}}」「{{salary_text}}」「{{region}}」.

Avoid:
- SVG, vector art, flat illustration, line art, icon-only banners, diagram-like panels, Figma/Canva template aesthetics, or geometric shapes as the main visual.
- A design that looks like a pure template made only from text boxes, arrows, icons, and flat colored shapes.
- Low contrast, broken Japanese typography, warped letters, excessive tiny labels, company logos, QR codes, URLs, or fake UI buttons.
- Making bonuses, prizes, gambling, or 「入社祝い金」 the main claim unless the actual post explicitly requires it.

Output requirements:
- Generate as a raster PNG image.
- Prioritize crisp text, smartphone readability, strong contrast, and a realistic or semi-real textured background.
