Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: 1:1 Japanese job recruitment banner for Jimoty, PNG raster image

Create a bright, high-energy Japanese job banner for 「{{role_phrase}}」 in 「{{region}}」.
The main visual should feel like a polished semi-real raster illustration or photo-real recruitment poster, with a friendly worker/person on the left side and a dense but readable text composition on the right side.

Required main copy:
- Largest priority text: 「{{salary_text}}」
- Role text: 「{{role_phrase}}」
- Area text: 「{{region}}」
- Short support labels may include: 「未経験OK」, 「安定収入」, 「自分のペースで働ける」

Layout and hierarchy:
- Square 1:1 composition with the person occupying roughly the left 35-45% of the image.
- Put 「{{salary_text}}」 in the largest type, centered-right or lower-right, with very high contrast and a thick white outline/drop shadow.
- Put 「{{role_phrase}}」 above the salary in a bold horizontal title band or brush-stroke panel, clearly smaller than salary but still readable on a phone.
- Use 「{{region}}」 as a compact location chip or small supporting line, not as the main headline.
- Add 3-4 small benefit chips along the bottom, each with very short Japanese text and simple supporting marks.
- Keep the total amount of text short; prioritize smartphone readability over filling every space.

Visual style:
- Energetic Japanese pop-ad design with hand-painted brush strokes, thick outlines, sticker-like labels, paper texture, subtle ink splatter, and layered highlights.
- Use a bright teal main color with coral/red accent, yellow marker underline accents, black text, and white outline/shadow for legibility.
- Typography should mix bold rounded Japanese display lettering, thick hand-drawn brush numerals, and compact readable sans-serif labels.
- Background should have realistic depth, lighting, texture, and raster detail; it must not look like a flat vector template.
-人物や作業場は写真風または半写実の実在感があり、清潔で信頼できる求人バナーの雰囲気にする。

Content rules:
- Make the job category obvious through the role text and subtle scene cues related to 「{{role_phrase}}」.
- Do not include a real company name, real service logo, LINE URL, QR code, watermark, or fake app logo.
- Do not make 「クリックして」, 「ボタンで」, 「LINEで」, or other strong CTA/button-style wording the main message.
- Do not contradict the job post text; keep claims general unless provided by placeholders.
- Do not use tiny text, broken Japanese typography, excessive long sentences, or unreadable decorative type.

Strict avoid:
- No SVG, vector art, flat illustration, line-art-only style, icon-driven banner, Figma/Canva template look, or geometric shapes-only composition.
- Even if the output file is PNG/JPG, it must not visually resemble a vector ad or a shape-only layout.
- Do not generate HTML, SVG, XML, or code-based artwork.
- Do not rely on icons or colored panels as the primary visual; realistic or semi-real raster people/backgrounds must carry the image.
