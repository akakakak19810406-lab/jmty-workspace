Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: 1:1 Japanese job recruitment banner for Jimoty remote-work listings

Create a bright, high-impact Japanese job banner for a fully remote role.
The design should feel like an energetic anime-style remote work recruitment ad: a cheerful headset support worker or home-office operator at a laptop, soft daylight in a modern room, clean desk items, and a friendly AI/support-work impression. Keep the job clearly remote, not factory-like.

Main text hierarchy:
- Top ribbon: 「完全在宅で、自分らしく働く！」 or a short equivalent that includes 「完全在宅」
- Main headline: 「{{role_phrase}}」 in very large Japanese lettering
- Salary block: 「{{salary_text}}」 must be the most visually dominant salary element, large, high contrast, and easy to read on a phone
- Region/condition line: include 「{{region}}」 naturally if the listing needs a location display
- Support copy: short phrases such as 「未経験OK」「PCがあればOK」「自由な働き方」「AIサポートあり」, only if they match the post content

Reusable copy direction:
- Use short Japanese phrases only; prioritize smartphone readability over filling space.
- Make 「完全在宅」 clearly visible in the upper area or a strong benefit card.
- Keep {{role_phrase}}, {{salary_text}}, and {{region}} consistent with the actual post text.
- For the role scene, show home PC work, chat support, data entry, online customer support, spreadsheet-like tasks, AI assistance, or clerical remote-work cues.

Visual style:
- Bright anime illustration blended with polished recruitment-banner graphics.
- Right side or center-right: friendly young remote worker wearing a headset, seated at a laptop in a sunny home office.
- Background: soft-focus home interior with daylight, plants, desk, laptop, and calm remote-work atmosphere.
- Color palette: vivid hot pink for key ribbons and salary panels, clean blue for trust/support accents, white panels for readability, yellow highlights for salary numbers and badges.
- Typography: bold rounded Japanese display type for headline and salary; white or dark navy outlines/shadows for contrast; avoid thin or decorative fonts for important text.
- Density: lively but organized, with clear zones for headline, salary, character, and benefits. Use sparkle accents sparingly.

Layout:
- Square 1:1 composition.
- Upper-left/top: pink ribbon containing the fully remote message.
- Left or center-left: large role headline with strong pink/blue contrast.
- Lower-left or middle-left: oversized salary block for {{salary_text}}, with 「月収」 or the salary label readable.
- Right side: illustrated remote worker at laptop, not blocking the salary text.
- Mid-lower area: compact support panel or checklist for 2-3 matching benefits.
- Bottom row: 3-5 simple icon benefit cards, for example 「完全在宅」「PCがあればOK」「自由な働き方」「未経験歓迎」「高収入を実現」, only where accurate.

Avoid:
- Do not use button-like CTA wording as the main appeal, such as 「クリックして」「ボタンで」「LINEで」「今すぐ応募」.
- Do not include LINE URLs, QR codes, real company names, logos, watermarks, or fake app UI.
- Do not make factory, manufacturing line, helmets, uniforms, warehouses, or physical labor the main subject.
- Do not use tiny body text, cluttered paragraphs, low contrast text, broken Japanese typography, or unreadable salary numbers.
- Do not contradict the post content for salary, region, role, or work conditions.

Output requirements:
- PNG-style final image.
- Text must be sharp, high contrast, and legible at smartphone size.
- The banner should sell the remote-work image itself, with {{salary_text}} and 「完全在宅」 as the strongest readable hooks.
