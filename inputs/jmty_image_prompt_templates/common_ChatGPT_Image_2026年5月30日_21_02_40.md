Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: 1:1 Japanese job recruitment banner for Jimoty, PNG raster image

Create a high-impact Japanese job banner for 「{{role_phrase}}」 in 「{{region}}」.
The visual should feel like a polished, photo-real or semi-real recruitment ad: energetic, modern, trustworthy, and readable on a smartphone.

Main text hierarchy:
- Most prominent text: 「{{salary_text}}」
- Secondary headline: 「{{role_phrase}}」
- Supporting label: 「{{region}}」
- Short benefit chips may include: 「未経験OK」「学歴不問」「研修サポート充実」 or other facts that match the post

Layout and composition:
- Square 1:1 composition with dense but controlled magazine-ad styling.
- Use a large photo-real or semi-real person or work scene as the main background, occupying much of the frame and adding depth, lighting, and real texture.
- Place 「{{salary_text}}」 very large in the lower-left or lower-center area, using bold white or high-contrast lettering with a strong shadow or dark backing so it is instantly readable.
- Place 「{{role_phrase}}」 as a bold Japanese headline on the right or upper-right side, balanced against the person or work scene.
- Add 「{{region}}」 as a smaller but clear tag, not competing with salary.
- Use short horizontal benefit chips near the bottom, separated by simple dividers, with enough spacing for mobile readability.
- Keep Japanese text concise. Do not crowd the image with long sentences.

Visual style:
- Bright cyber-pop recruitment banner look with a realistic photographic base.
- High contrast palette using deep navy/black, white, vivid cyan, hot pink, and small yellow accents.
- Add translucent geometric overlays, diagonal blocks, dotted texture, and thin line accents only as supporting design elements.
- Typography should be heavy, crisp, and modern, with large Japanese characters and clean numeric salary text.
- Make the salary text the first thing viewers notice, then the role, then the region and benefits.
- Text must be sharp, correctly spelled Japanese, with clean kerning and no warped or broken characters.

Content constraints:
- The title and labels must not contradict the source post.
- Do not include LINE URLs, QR codes, real company names, watermarks, fake logos, or app button UI.
- Do not make 「クリックして」「ボタンで」「LINEで」 or similar strong CTA/button-like wording the main message.
- If a short handwritten-style accent is used, keep it secondary and non-CTA, such as a mood line that does not promise unsupported conditions.

Avoid:
- SVG, vector art, flat illustration, line art, icon-led banners, Figma/Canva-style vector ad templates, or designs that look like they are made only from shapes.
- Even if the final file is PNG/JPG, do not make it look like a vector-only or geometry-only advertisement.
- Plain single-color backgrounds, paper-theater style flat panels, or layouts without realistic texture, lighting, and depth.
- Tiny text, low contrast text, unreadable Japanese, excessive small captions, QR codes, company logos, watermarks, and generic stock-ad blandness.
