Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: 1:1 Japanese remote job recruitment banner for Jimoty

Goal:
Create a vivid, high-impact Japanese job banner for a fully remote role. The image should feel energetic, polished, and social-ad ready, with the salary as the strongest visual hook and a clear work-from-home impression.

Required Japanese text:
- 「{{salary_text}}」
- 「完全在宅」
- 「{{role_phrase}}」
- 「未経験OK」
- 「{{region}}」

Optional short supporting copy:
- 「好きな時間に、好きな場所で」
- 「PC作業中心」
- 「オンライン対応」

Visual style:
Use a glossy, high-energy recruitment-banner look with bright pink and magenta as the main accent colors, white highlights, sparkling light streaks, diagonal motion lines, and paint-brush banner shapes. Add gold/yellow treatment to the salary number so it looks premium and immediately readable. The mood should be optimistic, modern, and slightly dramatic, but still clean enough for a job listing.

Layout:
- Square 1:1 composition.
- Put the largest salary text 「{{salary_text}}」 on the left or center-left, occupying the main visual area.
- Place 「完全在宅」 near the top or near the salary in a highly visible badge or bold headline strip.
- Put 「{{role_phrase}}」 as the second-largest text, below or beside the salary.
- Add 「未経験OK」 and 「{{region}}」 as smaller supporting labels, not competing with salary.
- Use a right-side or background scene of a modern home desk, laptop, spreadsheet-like work, online task screen, AI-assisted office work, or a friendly remote worker holding/using a laptop.
- Keep text short, layered, and readable on a smartphone.

Typography:
Use bold Japanese display typography with thick white outlines, black drop shadows, and hot-pink accent strokes. Salary numbers should be oversized, gold/yellow gradient, beveled or glossy, with strong contrast against the pink/white background. Supporting text can use bold white or black lettering with clear outlines. Avoid thin fonts and small decorative text.

Color and density:
Dominant colors: hot pink, magenta, white, gold/yellow, black shadow accents, and small teal or light-cyan highlights only if useful. Use dense promotional energy similar to a Japanese web ad: sparkles, speed lines, paint strokes, and diagonal bands. Keep the hierarchy controlled so the salary and 「完全在宅」 are immediately understood.

Remote-work scene direction:
Show work-from-home context, such as a clean home desk, laptop, documents, spreadsheet-like UI, online meeting or data-entry atmosphere, daylight room, and casual office clothing. The banner must read as remote work, data entry, AI support, online admin work, or PC-based office work.

Avoid:
- Do not include strong CTA button wording such as 「クリックして」, 「ボタンで」, 「LINEで」, or similar action-button copy as the main message.
- Do not include LINE URLs, QR codes, real company names, app logos, service logos, watermarks, or brand marks.
- Do not make it look like a factory job. Avoid factories, production lines, work uniforms, helmets, safety vests, machinery, warehouses, and manufacturing scenes.
- Do not use tiny text, low-contrast text, broken Japanese typography, cluttered unreadable labels, or misleading salary/region/condition text.
- Do not let decorative effects cover the salary, 「完全在宅」, or 「{{role_phrase}}」.

Output quality:
PNG-style crisp banner, sharp Japanese text, high contrast, smartphone-readable hierarchy, no contradiction with the job post. Match the placeholders exactly to the provided job content: role={{role_phrase}}, salary={{salary_text}}, region={{region}}.
