Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: 1:1 Japanese job recruitment banner for Jimoty, PNG raster image

Create a bright, friendly Japanese recruitment banner for {{region}}.
Main copy to include, with clear hierarchy:
- Largest priority text: 「{{salary_text}}」
- Main role text: 「{{role_phrase}}」
- Short trust/entry text: 「未経験OK」
- Optional small area text if space allows: 「{{region}}で働ける」「{{benefit_1}}」「{{benefit_2}}」

Visual style:
- Cheerful semi-realistic or high-quality 3D character style, not flat illustration.
- Use an approachable person holding a smartphone or working naturally, with warm facial expression.
- Background should feel like a real scene or semi-real location with depth: soft blue sky, warm sunlight, gentle bokeh, subtle workplace or town atmosphere, and natural lighting.
- Add a lively but polished recruitment mood with soft glow, shallow depth of field, and light texture.
- Decorative hearts, sparkles, and soft social-media-like shapes may appear only as blurred atmospheric accents, not as icon-led design.

Layout:
- Square 1:1 composition optimized for smartphone readability.
- Put {{salary_text}} in the upper-left or central-left area as the strongest visual anchor.
- Use very large, thick Japanese lettering with white outline, soft drop shadow, and high contrast.
- Place {{role_phrase}} near the salary as the second-largest text, keeping it short and easy to scan.
- Put 「未経験OK」 on a yellow or warm accent ribbon, brush label, or small banner below the main copy.
- Keep supporting benefit points to two or three short chips or rounded badges near the bottom, each with large readable text.
- Character should occupy the right side or lower-right side, overlapping the text area slightly without hiding any words.
- Leave enough safe margin around all text so it does not touch the image edge.

Typography and color:
- Use bold rounded Japanese display typography, clear white edging, and layered shadows.
- Salary text must be the most readable element, using vivid pink, coral, red-orange, or orange gradient with strong white outline.
- Use a cheerful palette: sky blue, sunlight yellow, coral pink, orange, turquoise, and clean white.
- Maintain high contrast between text and background; do not place thin text over busy areas.
- Keep text short, large, and layered by importance for mobile viewing.

Content rules:
- The image should sell the job appeal through salary, role, location, and beginner-friendly tone.
- Do not make action-button CTA language the main message.
- Do not include phrases such as 「クリックして」, 「ボタンで」, 「LINEで」, 「今すぐ応募」, or similar button-like CTA wording.
- Do not include LINE URL, QR code, real company name, official logo, watermark, phone number, or website URL.
- Do not contradict the job post text supplied with {{role_phrase}}, {{salary_text}}, and {{region}}.

Strict avoid:
- No SVG, vector art, flat illustration, line art, icon素材主体, Figma/Canva-like vector ad, or shape-only template design.
- Even if the output file is PNG/JPG, it must not look like a flat vector graphic or a layout made only from geometric shapes.
- Do not create HTML, SVG, XML, code, wireframe, mockup UI, or a purely graphic template.
- Avoid tiny text, dense paragraphs, broken Japanese typography, fake UI buttons, logos, QR codes, and unreadable decorative fonts.
- Text bands, ribbons, and chips are allowed only as support elements; the image must still have realistic lighting, depth, texture, and a person/scene presence.
