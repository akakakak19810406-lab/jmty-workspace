Use case: JMTY factory / manufacturing job recruitment banner
Asset type: 1:1 Japanese raster image banner, PNG output, smartphone-readable

Reusable prompt:
Create a bold Japanese job recruitment banner for a factory or light manufacturing role.

Main text to include:
- Largest priority text: 「{{salary_text}}」
- Job role text: 「{{role_phrase}}」
- Region text: 「{{region}}」
- Short support text if appropriate: 「未経験OK」「寮費無料」「土日休み」など、投稿文と一致する短い条件のみ

Visual style:
- Energetic, high-impact Japanese job-ad look with a strong black / yellow / red / white palette.
- Use a photo-real or semi-real worker in factory workwear as the main human subject, placed large on the left or foreground, with confident expression and dynamic pose.
- Show a believable factory or light manufacturing environment behind or around the subject: clean production line, inspection table, machinery, safety-conscious workspace, metal/plastic material textures, practical lighting, and depth.
- Salary must dominate the composition: very large bold Japanese typography, high contrast, preferably yellow or white on a dark area, with clear hierarchy so it is readable on a phone.
- Put {{role_phrase}} on a strong red or yellow diagonal band or solid label area, but keep it secondary to {{salary_text}}.
- Place {{region}} smaller than salary and role, as a clear supporting location label, not as the main headline.
- Use dense but controlled layout: dramatic diagonal angles, sharp contrast, limited accent shapes, distressed/grit texture on large letters, and a few triangular yellow accents only as supporting decoration.
- Text should feel like a printed raster job banner with photographic depth, not a flat vector template.

Typography and layout rules:
- Keep Japanese text short and layered: salary first, role second, region/conditions third.
- Use thick block-style Japanese lettering with crisp edges, strong shadows or outlines, and enough spacing to avoid crowding.
- Do not use tiny text. Avoid long sentences. Every visible word must be legible at small mobile size.
- Avoid contradiction with the post text. Do not invent salary, region, benefits, company names, or work conditions.

Factory-specific direction:
- Make it clearly factory / manufacturing / light-work recruitment, not remote work.
- Suitable role examples: 検査・組立スタッフ, 軽作業スタッフ, 製造スタッフ, 機械操作スタッフ.
- The scene should communicate hands-on work, equipment, safety, production, inspection, assembly, or machine operation.
- Do not make a home office, laptop-only work, side-job, smartphone-only job, or fully remote image.

Avoid:
- Do not make 「クリックして」「ボタンで」「LINEで」 or other button-like CTA text the main appeal.
- Do not include LINE URLs, QR codes, real company names, logos, watermarks, or fake UI buttons.
- Do not include 「完全在宅」「出勤不要」「スマホだけ」 or wording that suggests remote work.
- Do not create SVG, vector art, flat illustration, line art, icon-based banners, Figma/Canva-like geometric templates, or images made only from shapes and text.
- Even if the output file is PNG/JPG, the visual result must not look like a flat SVG/vector advertisement.
- Do not use a black solid background with only shapes and text; include photographic or semi-real texture, lighting, depth, and a real-feeling work environment.

Example placeholder context:
- {{region}} = 青葉県みなと市
- {{salary_text}} = 月収32万円可
- {{role_phrase}} = 検査・組立スタッフ
