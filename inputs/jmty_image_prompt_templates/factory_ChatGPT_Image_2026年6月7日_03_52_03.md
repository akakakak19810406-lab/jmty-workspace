Use case: JMTY factory / manufacturing job recruitment banner
Asset type: 1:1 Japanese raster image banner, PNG output, smartphone-readable

Reusable prompt:
Create a bold Japanese recruitment banner for a manufacturing / light-work role.
The image should communicate a high-condition job offer through typography and layout first.

Main text:
- Largest priority text: 「月収40万円」
- Second largest priority text: 「入社祝い金160万円」
- Job role text: 「{{role_phrase}}」
- Optional region text: 「{{region}}」
- Short support text if useful: 「未経験OK」

Visual direction:
- Use an energetic Japanese job-ad look with strong contrast and readable typography.
- Layout and art direction should follow the selected style. Pop SNS ad, premium magazine ad, 3D typography poster, Japanese flyer style, or minimal web ad are all acceptable.
- Factory, worker, workwear, helmet, machine, and production-line visuals are optional only. Do not make them mandatory.
- If job-related visuals are used, keep them clean and secondary so the two money claims stay dominant.

Typography and layout rules:
- Keep Japanese text short: money claims first, role second, region/benefit third.
- Do not add 「規定あり」「案件により異なります」 or other disclaimer text to the image.
- Do not use remote-work wording such as 「完全在宅」「出勤不要」「スマホだけ」.
- Do not use button-like CTA text such as 「クリックして」「ボタンで」「LINEで」「今すぐ応募」.

Avoid:
- LINE URLs, QR codes, real company names, logos, watermarks, fake UI buttons.
- SVG, vector art, flat illustration, line art, icon-based banners, Figma/Canva-like geometric templates, or images made only from shapes and text boxes.

Example placeholder context:
- {{region}} 青葉県みなと市
- {{role_phrase}} 検査・組立スタッフ

Output requirement:
Generate a square raster PNG image with crisp Japanese typography and high smartphone readability.
People / character direction:
- If the image uses a person or character, use a female staff member / female character as the main foreground subject, unless the selected art style intentionally uses a gender-ambiguous abstract character. Do not over-emphasize workwear or factory realism; keep her ad-friendly, approachable, and visually appealing.
