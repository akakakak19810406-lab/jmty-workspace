Create a high-impact Japanese recruitment banner for {{region}} using a photo-real or semi-realistic raster scene as the base. The design should feel like a dense, glossy Japanese job ad: energetic, readable on a phone, and built around a realistic work setting rather than flat shapes.

Main copy to include:
- 「{{salary_text}}」
- 「{{role_phrase}}」
- 「未経験OK」
- Optional short support copy if space allows: 「在宅で安定収入」 or 「サポートあり」

Visual style:
- Use a bright, trustworthy, high-contrast palette centered on deep royal blue, vivid red, white, and golden yellow.
- Make {{salary_text}} the largest and most important text, with thick 3D beveled lettering, glossy highlights, strong drop shadow, and a clear outline so it can be read immediately on a smartphone.
- Use {{role_phrase}} as the second visual anchor in bold red or white Japanese headline lettering with a dimensional, poster-like finish.
- Use blue header or side ribbons only as supporting structure, not as the whole image.
- Use small yellow circular or rectangular badges for concise trust/support points such as 「未経験OK」 or 「サポートあり」.

Scene and background:
- Show a believable Japanese home-office or clean work environment with depth, natural light, desk items, monitor, laptop, or work tools related to {{role_phrase}}.
- If a person appears, make them approachable and realistic, working naturally in the scene; avoid celebrity likenesses and avoid overly polished stock-photo posing.
- The background must have real texture, lighting, perspective, and depth. Text panels and ribbons should overlay the raster scene without turning the image into a flat template.

Layout:
- Square 1:1 composition with a busy but controlled Japanese ad density.
- Top area: short category or lifestyle phrase, for example 「{{region}}で働ける」 or 「好きな時間に働ける」, kept smaller than the salary.
- Center-left or center: dominant {{salary_text}}.
- Center or upper center: {{role_phrase}} headline.
- Lower area: 2-3 compact benefit boxes with short text only, such as 「仕事内容」「働き方」「サポート」.
- Keep all Japanese text large, bold, outlined, and separated into clear hierarchy. Avoid long sentences.

Text rules:
- Prioritize {{salary_text}} above all other claims.
- Use short Japanese phrases only; every phrase must be readable on a phone.
- Do not include LINE URLs, QR codes, real company names, watermarks, or logos.

Avoid:
- SVG, vector art, flat illustration, line art, icon-only layouts, Figma/Canva-style geometric ad templates, or a design made only from panels and shapes.
- Tiny text, low contrast text, broken Japanese typography, excessive small bullet points, QR codes, company logos, fake app screens with unreadable details, and clutter that hides {{salary_text}}.
- A final image that looks like HTML/SVG/XML/code-generated artwork instead of a raster PNG/JPG style banner.

Output expectation:
- Final visual should look like a polished raster job banner with photographic or semi-realistic depth, crisp high-contrast Japanese typography, and {{salary_text}} as the first thing viewers notice.
