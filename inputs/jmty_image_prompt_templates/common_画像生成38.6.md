Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: 1:1 Japanese job recruitment banner for Jimoty, raster PNG output

Primary request:
Create a high-impact Japanese job banner for {{region}} using short, readable text:
「{{role_phrase}}」
「{{salary_text}}」
「未経験OK」

Visual style:
Energetic shonen manga advertisement style with a semi-realistic raster illustration base. Use bold comic speed lines radiating from the background, dynamic black brush strokes behind key text, thick ink-like outlines, halftone shading, dramatic contrast, and a sense of motion. The scene should feel intense, youthful, and eye-catching, but still suitable for a job recruitment post.

Main scene:
Show an approachable worker related to {{role_phrase}} in an active work moment, with tools, desk, device, workspace, or job-relevant props that make the role understandable. Use semi-realistic manga rendering with visible texture, lighting, depth, and raster brush detail. The background may include monochrome manga line art or workplace details, but it must not become a flat vector pattern.

Layout:
Use a dense square composition similar to a manga magazine ad. Place the largest headline across the top, the salary block as the most prominent readable element near the upper-left or center-left, and the role / benefit text beside it in a strong horizontal band. Put the character or work scene in the lower half, overlapping the text area slightly without hiding any words. Keep all text short and separated into clear visual levels for smartphone readability.

Typography:
Use oversized Japanese display lettering with heavy black outer stroke, bright inner fill, and high-contrast shadow. Make {{salary_text}} the clearest and largest priority after or equal to the headline. Suggested color treatment: red or orange headline with yellow glow and black outline; {{salary_text}} in white-to-mint or bright green gradient with black outline; support text in white or pale green on black brush-stroke backing. Avoid thin fonts and small decorative captions.

Color and density:
Use a white or light manga-paper base with black speed lines, red/orange/yellow headline accents, green salary accents, and strong black outlines. The image should feel busy and explosive, but text must remain crisp. Keep decorative effects behind the words, never over the words.

Required text hierarchy:
1. {{salary_text}} must be large, high contrast, and immediately readable.
2. {{role_phrase}} must be clear and consistent with the job post.
3. 「未経験OK」 may appear as a strong secondary benefit.
4. {{region}} may appear only if it fits naturally and does not compete with the salary.

Avoid:
Do not put strong CTA wording such as 「クリックして」, 「ボタンで」, 「LINEで」, 「今すぐ応募」, or button-like call-to-action text as the main appeal. Do not include a LINE URL, QR code, real company name, logo, watermark, fake UI button, or tiny unreadable text. Do not contradict the job post headline.

Strict raster-image rule:
Do not create an SVG, vector-art, flat illustration, line-icon, infographic, Canva/Figma template, geometric panel ad, or shape-only composition. Even if the final file is PNG or JPG, the visible result must not look like vector advertising. The background, person, and workspace must have photographic or semi-realistic raster texture, lighting, depth, and hand-drawn manga detail. Text bars and chips are allowed only as supporting elements.

Output requirements:
Generate a square PNG-style banner image. Keep Japanese typography sharp, correctly spelled, and readable on a phone. Prioritize the visual appeal of the banner itself over CTA-style instruction text.
