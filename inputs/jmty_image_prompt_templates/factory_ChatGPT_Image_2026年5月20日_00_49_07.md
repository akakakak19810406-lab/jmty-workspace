Avoid thin fonts, long sentences, tiny captions, decorative unreadable script, or distorted Japanese characters.

Base the image on deep navy, bright factory blue, white, and vivid gold/yellow. Add small accents of red or orange as paint strokes or energetic highlights, but do not let them overpower the salary and headline.
