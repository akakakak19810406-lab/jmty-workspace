Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: 1:1 Japanese job recruitment banner for Jimoty, PNG output

Reusable prompt:
Create a square Japanese recruitment banner for a factory / manufacturing job.
Use a dramatic premium poster style inspired by bold Japanese magazine ads: high-density composition, sharp diagonal blocks, layered paint splashes, rough brush textures, metallic gold accents, deep navy / black contrast, clean white panels, and selective red emphasis for the most important pay information.

Main text to include:
- Region label: 「{{region}}」
- Job category ribbon: 「工場求人」
- Role phrase: 「{{role_phrase}}」
- Salary text: 「{{salary_text}}」
- Trust point: 「未経験OK」
- Optional support points if provided: 「{{benefit_1}}」「{{benefit_2}}」「{{benefit_3}}」

Text hierarchy:
- Make 「{{salary_text}}」 the largest and highest-contrast element in the image. Use bold red or deep navy lettering with a thick white outline and subtle shadow so it remains readable on a phone.
- Make 「{{role_phrase}}」 the second strongest element, using large white Japanese display type on a dark angled ribbon.
- Keep 「{{region}}」 smaller but visible near the top-left, like a location tag.
- Keep benefit text short, separated into two or three boxed callouts. Good examples: 「寮費無料」「土日休み」「日払い相談可」「研修あり」「空調完備」. Use only benefits that match the post content.
- Avoid long sentences. Prefer short stacked phrases with clear spacing.

Visual layout:
- 1:1 square banner with a dynamic diagonal split.
- Left / center area: bold Japanese headline and salary block over a textured gold and dark navy background.
- Right area: clean illustrated factory scene or factory worker portrait. Show a manufacturing workplace, clean light industrial workspace, inspection / assembly line, machinery, safety-conscious atmosphere, work gloves, uniform, helmet, or protective eyewear where appropriate.
- Add two white rectangular information panels on the right or lower-right with simple navy circular icons, but keep them readable and uncluttered.
- Add a dark bottom strip with three compact benefit icons and short labels if the provided benefits fit.
- Use thick outlines, drop shadows, brush-stroke underline accents, and paper / ink texture, while keeping all Japanese text crisp.

Color and typography:
- Palette: deep navy, black, warm metallic gold, white, and controlled red accents.
- Use red mainly for salary or urgent pay emphasis, not for a clickable button.
- Use bold Mincho-style or strong Japanese display lettering for major phrases, and clean Gothic-style Japanese type for smaller benefits.
- Text must be sharp, high-contrast, and large enough for smartphone viewing.

Factory-specific direction:
- The image must clearly look like a factory, manufacturing, light work, inspection, assembly, or machine-operation job banner.
- Do not make remote work, home work, smartphone-only work, side-job income, laptop work, or at-home lifestyle the main subject.
- Do not include phrases such as 「完全在宅」「出勤不要」「スマホだけ」.
- Match all conditions to the post content: salary, region, holidays, dormitory support, and other benefits must not contradict the job post.

Avoid:
- Do not include LINE URLs, QR codes, real company names, real logos, watermarks, or fake app buttons.
- Do not use strong CTA phrases as the main appeal, such as 「クリックして」「ボタンで」「LINEで」.
- Do not place 「今すぐ応募」 or similar button-like CTA as the main visual focus.
- Do not use tiny text, broken Japanese typography, unreadable decorative fonts, low contrast, messy overlapping text, or excessive small copy.
- Do not leave the result as a generic reference-image instruction; apply the described style directly to the new job banner.
